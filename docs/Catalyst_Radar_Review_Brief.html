Catalyst Radar v1.1.1 review brief

Market radar, not autopilot.

The sharpened specs define a deterministic-first tool for finding public-equity candidates, forcing evidence discipline, and escalating only the strongest candidates into manual buy review.

Date: May 9, 2026 Scope: U.S.-listed equities and ADRs Highest automated state: Manual Buy Review Premium LLM calls: gated and budgeted

What, Why, How

What

A research radar that scans liquid equities, ranks candidates, applies hard risk blocks, and produces auditable Decision Cards when evidence is strong enough.

Why

Early market setups are usually scattered across price strength, volume, options, filings, news, earnings language, and sector read-throughs. The tool should reduce noise and improve timing discipline.

How

Use deterministic market math first, local text intelligence second, policy gates third, and sparse LLM synthesis only for candidates that already look action-relevant.

Core Cascade

S0Universe builder filters liquid, covered equities.
S1Deterministic market scan ranks price, volume, liquidity, volatility.
S2Event detector normalizes filings, news, earnings, revisions.
S3Local NLP scores source quality, theme match, sentiment, novelty.
S4Scoring and policy gates assign states and hard blocks.
S5Small or mid model reviews evidence and produces bear case.
S6GPT-5.5 creates Decision Card only after all gates pass.

Action States

WarningMultiple pillars improving.Run skeptic/research.

Decisions Needed From You

Dashboard

Choose Streamlit for speed or Next.js for a more polished long-term product surface.

Data providers

Choose free/cheap bootstrap providers first or paid feeds earlier for quality and completeness.

Universe

Start with a curated liquid universe for fast validation or the full liquid U.S. universe from the beginning.

Alerts

Dashboard only, email, Telegram, Slack, or webhooks.

Risk defaults

Confirm per-trade risk, max single-name exposure, sector/theme caps, and liquidity floor.

Hosting

Local Windows dev, Docker Compose, or cloud VM for always-on operation.

Capital-use gate The specs require shadow mode, paper trading, point-in-time backtests, leakage checks, baseline comparisons, useful-alert tracking, and cost review before any real-capital workflow.
